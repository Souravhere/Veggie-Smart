Introduction

The Advanced Catering Calculator helps event planners estimate the amount of food and drinks required based on the number of guests and selected menu options. This tool is designed to streamline the catering planning process for events such as weddings, parties, and conferences, providing flexibility with customizable food and drink selections.

Features

  • Calculate food and drink quantities based on the number of guests.
  • Choose from predefined meal and drink options or customize menus.
  • Dynamic cost estimation based on selected options.
  • User-friendly interface with responsive design.
  • Estimate costs in Indian Rupees (₹).

Usage

To use the Advanced Catering Calculator:

  1. Enter the number of guests attending the event.
  2. Select a food option from Basic Meal, Premium Meal, or Custom Menu.
  3. Select a drink option from Standard Drinks, Premium Drinks, or Custom Drinks.
  4. Click the Calculate button to estimate food and drink quantities.
  5. View the results displaying the total units of food and drinks needed and the estimated cost in Indian Rupees (₹).
