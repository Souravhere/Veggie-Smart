Land Measurement Tool

How to Use

  • Use the drawing tools on the map to outline the area of the land.
  • Click on the "Calculate Area" button to get the measurement.
  • Use the search box to find a specific location.

Note: Beta Version Disclaimer

This Land Measurement Tool is currently in beta and may not provide 100% accurate measurements. Use it for general reference and consult professional surveys for precise measurements.
