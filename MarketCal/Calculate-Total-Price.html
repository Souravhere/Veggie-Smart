Calculate Total Price

User Documentation for "Calculate Total Price" Calculator

Overview

The "Calculate Total Price" calculator helps you determine the total price of a product based on its price per kilogram and the quantity (in kilograms and grams) you wish to purchase.

Features

  • Input field for the price per kilogram (₹/kg).
  • Input fields for entering quantity in kilograms and grams.
  • Displays the calculated total price.
  • Clean and responsive design using Tailwind CSS classes.

How to Use the Calculator

Open the Calculator Page

Navigate to the calculator page in your browser.

Input the Price per Kilogram

Locate the input field labeled "Price (₹/kg):". Enter the price per kilogram of the product.

Input the Quantity

Locate the input fields labeled "Quantity (kg):" and "Quantity (g):". Enter the total quantity you wish to purchase in kilograms and grams.

Calculate the Total Price

Click the "Calculate Total" button. The calculator will process the inputs and display the total price in the result area.

View the Result

The calculated total price will be displayed below the form in the result area.
