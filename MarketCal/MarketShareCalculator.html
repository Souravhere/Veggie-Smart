Market Share Calculator

Detailed Documentation

The Market Share Calculator helps businesses determine their market share based on their sales and the total market sales within an industry. This tool is tailored for both Indian and international markets.

  • Select Country: Choose between India and other countries to account for market-specific conditions.
  • Company Sales: Enter your company's sales in the local currency.
  • Total Market Sales: Enter the total market sales in the local currency.

Calculation Method:

  • Market Share Percentage: Calculates the market share as a percentage of the company's sales to the total market sales.
  • Profit Amount: Computes the profit amount based on the company's sales and market share percentage.

Example Usage:

  • Company Sales: 1,000,000 (in local currency)
  • Total Market Sales: 5,000,000 (in local currency)
  • Market Share: 20%
  • Profit Amount: 200,000 (in local currency)
