User Documentation for "Calculate Price per Kg" Calculator

Overview

The "Calculate Price per Kg" calculator is a user-friendly tool designed to help you determine the price per kilogram of any product based on the total quantity (in kilograms and grams) and the total price. This tool is especially useful for shoppers, sellers, and anyone needing to convert bulk prices into a per-kilogram rate.
