Profit Margin Calculator

Detailed Documentation

The Profit Margin Calculator helps businesses determine the profit margin for products. Profit margin is the percentage of profit earned on a product relative to its cost price.

  • Cost Price: Enter the cost price of the product.
  • Selling Price: Enter the selling price of the product.
  • Calculation: The profit margin is calculated using the formula: ((Selling Price - Cost Price) / Selling Price) * 100.

The formula used is:
Profit Margin (%) = ((Selling Price - Cost Price) / Selling Price) * 100
