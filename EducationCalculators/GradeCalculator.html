Advanced Grade Calculator

How to Use This Calculator

Enter your assignment scores and their respective weights to calculate your final grade. Use the "Add Assignment" button to include more assignments.

Understanding the Grade Calculation

Formulas and Logic

The final grade is calculated using the weighted average formula:

finalGrade = (score1 * weight1 + score2 * weight2 + ... + scoreN * weightN) / (weight1 + weight2 + ... + weightN)

Each score is multiplied by its weight, and the sum of these products is divided by the sum of the weights.
