Ideal Weight Calculator

Detailed Documentation

The Ideal Weight Calculator determines the ideal weight based on height, age, and gender using the Devine formula. This formula is widely used in the medical field to estimate the ideal body weight.

  • Gender: Select your gender from the dropdown menu.
  • Age: Enter your age in years.
  • Height: Enter your height and select the appropriate unit (cm, m, or ft).
  • Calculation: The ideal weight is calculated using the Devine formula, adjusted for age.

The formula used is:
- For males: 50 + 0.9 * (height in cm - 152)
- For females: 45.5 + 0.9 * (height in cm - 152)
An age adjustment is applied: + 0.2 * (age - 40) for ages above 40.
