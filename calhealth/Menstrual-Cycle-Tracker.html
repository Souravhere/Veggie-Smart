Menstrual Cycle Tracker Calculator

Detailed Documentation

The Menstrual Cycle Tracker Calculator helps users predict periods, ovulation, and track symptoms efficiently.

  • Cycle Start Date: The date when the last menstrual period began.
  • Cycle Length: The average length of the menstrual cycle (e.g., 28 days).
  • Period Length: The average duration of the menstrual period (e.g., 5 days).
  • Optional Inputs: Symptoms, mood changes, flow intensity, and any notes related to the cycle.

Cycle Tracking:

  • Period Prediction: Predicts the start and end dates of future menstrual periods.
  • Cycle Overview: Provides a visual calendar or timeline showing past cycles and predicted future cycles.

Ovulation Prediction:

  • Ovulation Date: Estimates the ovulation day, typically around 14 days before the next expected period for a 28-day cycle.
  • Fertile Window: Calculates the fertile window, including the ovulation day and the five days leading up to it.

Symptom Tracking:

  • Daily Logs: Allows users to log daily symptoms, mood changes, and flow intensity.
  • Symptom Analysis: Analyzes logged symptoms to provide insights into how the menstrual cycle affects the user's health and mood.

Notifications and Reminders:

  • Upcoming Period: Sends notifications a few days before the predicted start of the next period.
  • Ovulation Reminder: Notifies users about the upcoming fertile window and predicted ovulation day.

Health Insights:

  • Cycle Regularity: Tracks the regularity of the menstrual cycle over time.
  • Symptom Patterns: Provides insights into recurring symptoms and their possible connections to different phases of the cycle.
  • Health Tips: Offers health tips based on the user's cycle patterns and symptoms.

Data Export:

  • Report Generation: Allows users to generate reports of their cycle history, symptoms, and other logged data.
  • Privacy Controls: Ensures users can securely manage their data, including options for data export and deletion.
