Hydration Calculator

This calculator helps determine your daily water intake requirements based on your age, weight, and activity level.

1. Enter your age in years.

2. Enter your weight in kilograms.

3. Select your activity level.

4. Click "Calculate Daily Water Intake".

Activity Level Description
Sedentary Little or no exercise
Light Light exercise/sports 1-3 days/week
Moderate Moderate exercise/sports 3-5 days/week
Active Hard exercise/sports 6-7 days a week
Very Active Very hard exercise/physical job
Super Active Professional athlete

Note: Beta Version Disclaimer

This Hydration Calculator is currently in beta and may not provide 100% accurate measurements. Use it for general reference and consult professional advice for precise recommendations.
