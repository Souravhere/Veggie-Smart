Calorie Burn Calculator

This calculator helps estimate the number of calories burned during different physical activities based on your weight and the duration of the activity.

1. Select the physical activity from the dropdown.

2. Enter your weight in kilograms.

3. Enter the duration of the activity in minutes.

4. Click "Calculate Calories Burned".
