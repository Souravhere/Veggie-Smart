About Us

Welcome to Veggie Smart, your ultimate platform for a wide array of calculators designed to make your life easier. Whether you are looking to calculate market prices, manage your health metrics, plan your finances, or even organize an event, we have the tools you need. Our mission is to transform complex calculations into simple and accessible solutions for everyone.

At Veggie Smart, we offer calculators in various streams including:

  • Market Pro Calculators: Calculate total price, price per kilogram, quantity, and discounts.
  • Health Calculators: BMI, calorie burn, hydration, and macronutrient calculators.
  • Finance Calculators: Loan, investment return, savings goal, budget planner, simple and compound interest calculators.
  • Home Improvement Calculators: Paint, garden area, advanced flooring, and renovation cost estimators.
  • Education Calculators: Percentage, advanced grade, study time planner, and tuition fee calculators.
  • Fitness Calculators: Workout plan generator, body fat percentage, one-rep max, and advanced pace calculators.
  • Automotive Calculators: Fuel cost, maintenance cost, vehicle loan, and depreciation cost calculators.
  • Event Planning Calculators: Guest list, budget planner, catering, and venue capacity calculators.
  • Food Calculators: Recipe cost, nutrition, and serving size converters.

Developed by Sourav Chhimpa, a dedicated web developer from India, Veggie Smart is built with the latest technologies to ensure a seamless and efficient user experience. Our calculators are crafted with precision using the following libraries and technologies:

  • Chart.js: For dynamic and interactive charts.
  • Babylon.js: For stunning 3D visualizations.
  • pdfmake: For generating PDF documents.
  • Tailwind CSS: For beautiful and responsive designs.
  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: The core building blocks of our web application.
  • Git: For version control and collaboration.
  • Special thanks to Icons8 for providing the icons used in our application.

We are continuously working on adding new calculators and enhancing the existing ones to serve you better. Stay tuned for more exciting features and updates in the future. Your feedback is crucial to us, so please do not hesitate to share your thoughts and suggestions.
