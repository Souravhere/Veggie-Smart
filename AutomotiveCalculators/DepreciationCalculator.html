Depreciation Calculator Documentation

The Advanced Depreciation Calculator estimates the depreciation of a vehicle over time based on user-provided information such as initial cost, residual value, useful life, depreciation method, and payment frequency.

Usage

  1. Enter the initial cost of the vehicle.
  2. Enter the residual value (estimated value at the end of its useful life).
  3. Enter the useful life of the vehicle in years.
  4. Select the depreciation method (Straight Line or Double Declining Balance).
  5. Select the payment frequency (Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, or Yearly).
  6. Click "Calculate Depreciation" to see the estimated depreciation amount.
  7. Optionally, download a PDF report for record-keeping.
