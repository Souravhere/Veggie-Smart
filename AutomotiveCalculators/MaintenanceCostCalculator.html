Maintenance Cost Calculator

What is this calculator?

The Maintenance Cost Calculator helps you track and estimate the cost of vehicle maintenance. By entering your maintenance activities and their costs, you can keep a record of your expenses and plan for future maintenance needs.

Maintenance Cost Calculator Documentation

The Maintenance Cost Calculator allows you to keep track of your vehicle maintenance expenses. It is a useful tool for budgeting and ensuring that you are aware of your vehicle's maintenance needs and costs.

Usage

  1. Enter the type of maintenance activity (e.g., Oil Change).
  2. Enter the cost of the maintenance in Rupees.
  3. Select the date of the maintenance.
  4. Click "Add Maintenance" to record the maintenance activity.
  5. Review the maintenance records and total cost displayed in the table.
  6. Optionally, download a PDF report for record-keeping.
