Understanding the Paint Calculation

Formula: The total area to be painted is calculated using the formula:

Total Area = 2 × (Length × Height + Width × Height) + Length × Width

This formula accounts for the walls and the ceiling of a rectangular room. The amount of paint needed is calculated based on an average paint coverage rate (typically 1 liter covers 10 square meters).
