How to Use the Discount Calculator

This Discount Calculator helps you determine the final price of an item after applying a discount. To use the calculator, follow these steps:

  1. Enter Total Price: Input the original price of the item in the "Total Price (₹)" field. This should be the amount before any discount is applied.
  2. Enter Discount Percentage: Input the discount percentage in the "Discount (%)" field. This is the percentage of the total price that will be deducted.
  3. Calculate Discount: Click the "Calculate Discount" button to see the discounted price. The result will be displayed below the button.

The discounted price will be shown in the result section, reflecting the amount you will pay after applying the discount.
